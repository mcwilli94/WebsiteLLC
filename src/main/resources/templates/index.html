Introduction


Lisl King Williams, LLC is a private practice run by me, Attorney Lisl King Williams. I practice exclusively in the State of Missouri. It is my practice to keep each client involved in their own cases and to explain each and every step to them. I personally handles all my clients' cases, my goal being to keep her clients' informed and involved in every step of their legal proceedings. My areas of legal practice include Domestic Relations, Municipal Cases, and Criminal Cases.

Domestic Relations is an area of law which involves divorce, paternity, non paternity, child support establishment and modifications, criminal non support, and custody and visitation.

Municipal is an area of law which involves traffic matters. This includes recalling warrants, setting up court dates, and settling cases. My focus is on father's rights. I have successfully defended fathers rights at the Appellate court level, I have provided testimony before the state legislature in support of expunging criminal non support convictions. Please see testimonials and interview websites.


Background


Attorney Williams graduated JD from Northwestern University School of Law and obtained a Masters in Management from JL Kellogg Graduate School Of Management 1981. Attorney Williams also served as a Municipal Court judge for nine years in the city of St. Louis.
