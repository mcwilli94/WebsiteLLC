Professional Philosophy


My professional philosophy is to treat all clients the way I would want to be treated and to accept my clients where they are without judgment. I realize that this is a very emotional and stressful time for my clients and I strive to be understanding of their situation. I personally believe that it is in every child’s interest to have two viable parents in their lives, however, I recognize that that is not always possible. Nevertheless, no parent regardless of their personal situation should be without some sort of contact with their children and I advocate for my clients to have a meaningful relationship with their children.
